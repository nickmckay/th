SOUND EFFECTS NEEDED FOR FISHING GAME
======================================

Download these 3 sound effects and save them in this folder:

1. cast.mp3
   - When: Plays when bait is dropped into water
   - Type: Splash, water plop, or fishing line cast sound
   - Suggested search: "water splash", "fishing cast", "plop"

2. catch_fish.mp3
   - When: Plays when catching a fish (success!)
   - Type: Success chime, happy sound, achievement bell
   - Suggested search: "success chime", "achievement", "win sound", "ding"

3. catch_trash.mp3
   - When: Plays when catching trash (fail)
   - Type: Bonk, fail sound, disappointed sound
   - Suggested search: "fail sound", "bonk", "error sound", "buzzer"
